Exhibition Road Drawing Resident: Liam O’Connor

Liam has been our the Exhibition Road Drawing Resident between April 2014 to April 2015. He has been documenting the transformation that has been taking place as the V&A reworks the entrance to the building from Exhibtion Road and adds brand new gallery space.

During the course of his residency Liam has been sketching the building progress on a 12 metre scroll to capture ideas, and spark thoughts. Here is the full illustration which you can explore and hear Liam talking about the drawing and the projects that the building work has inspired.
